About Us

Your Comfortable Home Away From Home

Welcome to Arkitexx Boarding House, where we provide comfortable and affordable accommodation for students and professionals in Central Visayas, Philippines.

Our boarding house is strategically located near major schools, making it convenient for students to commute. We pride ourselves on maintaining a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for all our residents.

House Rules

  • Check-in time: 2:00 PM | Check-out time: 12:00 PM
  • No loud noise after 10:00 PM
  • Visitors allowed until 8:00 PM
  • Keep common areas clean
  • No pets allowed
  • Respect fellow residents and staff
